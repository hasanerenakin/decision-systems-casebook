# Stakeholder Analysis

| Stakeholder | Goal | Main Concern | System Interaction |
|---|---|---|---|
| Operations manager | Improve service efficiency | High operating cost | Reviews recommendations |
| Dispatcher | Manage daily vehicle allocation | Unexpected disruptions | Updates assignments |
| Drivers | Complete routes safely and on time | Unrealistic schedules | Records trip events |
| Students | Reach campus reliably | Delays and overcrowding | Views schedules and submits feedback |
| Administration | Control budget and service quality | Lack of measurable results | Reviews KPI reports |
| IT team | Maintain the platform | Integration and security | Manages system services |

## Stakeholder Conflicts

- Students prefer frequent services, while administration wants lower costs.
- Dispatchers need flexibility, while management wants standardized rules.
- Drivers may report operational causes that are not visible in schedule data.
- Management may prioritize utilization while students prioritize comfort.
