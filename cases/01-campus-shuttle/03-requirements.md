# System Requirements

## Functional Requirements

### FR-001 Route Management

The system shall allow authorized users to create, update and deactivate
shuttle routes.

### FR-002 Trip Scheduling

The system shall store planned departure and arrival times for each trip.

### FR-003 Capacity Recording

The system shall record vehicle capacity and estimated passenger count.

### FR-004 Delay Tracking

The system shall calculate the difference between planned and actual
arrival times.

### FR-005 Utilization Calculation

The system shall calculate capacity utilization for each completed trip.

### FR-006 Recommendation Generation

The system shall generate vehicle allocation recommendations using
capacity, demand and delay rules.

### FR-007 Alert Management

The system shall create alerts when operational thresholds are exceeded.

### FR-008 Feedback Connection

The system shall associate student feedback with routes, stops and trips.

## Non-Functional Requirements

### NFR-001 Explainability

Every recommendation shall include the rule and measurements that produced it.

### NFR-002 Auditability

Changes to route schedules and vehicle assignments shall be recorded.

### NFR-003 Availability

The management interface should be available during transportation
operating hours.

### NFR-004 Security

Only authorized staff shall modify routes, schedules and recommendation rules.

### NFR-005 Performance

Route performance reports should be generated within an acceptable response time.
